Step 1 of 11 Data Import

Import your data!

TADA accepts spreadsheets in the .csv format from the Water Quality Portal or any .csv formatted in the Water Quality eXchange (WQX) format (i.e., WQX required column headers). Use the features on this screen to upload your data. For questions or assistance using TADA, please contact the TADA team at mullin.cristina@epa.gov and include the phrase "TADA Data Discovery and Cleaning" in the subject line.

Flagged Sites and Results

We reviewed and appended columns to your dataset to flag results that may be inacurate, uncommon, or not relevant. You can remove data from your dataset that you do not need using the toggles below. If you decide not to remove flagged data, the flag columns we added to perform this check will still be available in the spreadsheet that you can download.
